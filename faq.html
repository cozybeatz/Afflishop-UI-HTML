Frequently Asked Questions

What is Afflishop?

Afflishop is a digital products marketplace, providing steady income for vendors and affiliates.

What is a digital product ?

A digital product is simply a non-physical asset or service that can be sold, purchased and consumed online. Digital products includes but not limited to Courses, Ebooks, Memberships, Coaching and Tools.

Who is a vendor?

A vendor is the creator/owner of the digital products.

Who is an affiliate?

An Affiliate is a person who engages in the business of affiliate marketing.

What is affiliate marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a type of performance-based marketing where individuals or companies promote products or services of other businesses and earn a commission for each sale or lead they generate. Affiliates (also known as publishers) typically use various marketing techniques such as websites, blogs, social media, or email marketing to drive traffic to the merchant's website and encourage conversions.

How does affiliate marketing work?

Affiliate marketing involves three primary parties: the merchant (seller), the affiliate (promoter), and the customer. The affiliate promotes the merchant's products or services through their marketing channels, using special tracking links or codes provided by the merchant. When a customer clicks on the affiliate's link, visits the merchant's website, and makes a purchase, the affiliate receives a commission based on the agreed-upon terms.

How do affiliates earn money?

Affiliates earn money through commissions. When a customer makes a purchase or performs a desired action (such as signing up for a service or filling out a form) through the affiliate's referral link, the affiliate receives a percentage of the sale or a fixed commission, depending on the affiliate program's terms. Commissions can vary widely, ranging from a few percent to a significant portion of the product's price.

What are the benefits of affiliate marketing?

Affiliate marketing offers several benefits for all parties involved. For merchants, it provides an effective way to reach a wider audience and increase sales without significant upfront investment. Affiliates can earn passive income by promoting products they believe in, without the need to create their own products. Customers benefit from the personalized recommendations and additional information provided by affiliates, making their purchase decisions easier.

Are there any costs associated with affiliate marketing?

While affiliate marketing can be relatively low-cost compared to other business models, there are some expenses to consider. These may include website hosting, domain registration, marketing tools, advertising costs, content creation, and other promotional expenses. However, the specific costs can vary greatly depending on your chosen approach and the scale of your affiliate marketing activities.

How do affiliates track their referrals and commissions?

Affiliates typically use unique affiliate links or codes provided by the merchant to track their referrals and commissions accurately. When a customer clicks on an affiliate link, a tracking cookie is placed on their device, allowing the merchant to identify the affiliate responsible for the referral. Some affiliate networks and platforms also provide comprehensive dashboards and reporting tools for affiliates to monitor their performance, track conversions, and view their earnings.

Can I do affiliate marketing without a website?

While having a website or online platform can greatly facilitate your affiliate marketing efforts, it is not always mandatory. Some affiliates leverage social media platforms, YouTube channels, email marketing, or other channels to promote affiliate products without a website. However, having a dedicated website or blog provides a more stable and customizable platform to showcase your content, build an audience, and establish your brand.

How can I become an affiliate marketer?

To become an affiliate marketer, you can follow these steps:

  • Choose a niche or industry you are passionate about.
  • Research and identify affiliate programs within your chosen niche.
  • Join affiliate networks or directly sign up with individual merchants.
  • Create a platform or online presence to promote products (e.g., website, blog, social media).
  • Produce high-quality content and incorporate affiliate links strategically.
  • Drive traffic to your platform through various marketing techniques.
  • Monitor your performance, analyze data, and optimize your strategies for better results.
